FAQ  

Q1: Is the $169 price still valid after Prime Day?  

A1: Amazon’s pricing is dynamic. The $169 price was a limited‑time offer tied to inventory levels. Post‑Prime Day, prices may rise back toward MSRP.

Q2: Can I return the AirPods Pro 3 if I’m not satisfied?  

A2: Yes, Amazon offers a 30‑day return policy for most products, including AirPods Pro 3.

Q3: Do I need an Apple ID to use these earbuds?  

A3: No, the earbuds are Bluetooth-enabled and work with any device that supports Bluetooth 5.3. An Apple ID is only required for ecosystem features like Siri, Apple Music, and Find My.

Q4: How does the ANC performance compare to the original AirPods Pro?  

A4: The AirPods Pro 3’s ANC offers up to 2 dB better attenuation at low frequencies and improved speech clarity, thanks to the H2 chip.

Q5: Can I pair the earbuds with a non‑Apple device?  

A5: Absolutely. They pair seamlessly with Android phones, Windows PCs, and even gaming consoles that support Bluetooth audio.
